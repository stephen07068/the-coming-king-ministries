Welcome Home From the General Overseer

A Message of Hope

Welcome to The Coming King Ministries. Our mission is clear: to equip believers with the truth of the Word and prepare a glorious church without spot or wrinkle. We believe in the power of holiness and the urgency of the harvest.

Whether you are a seeker looking for truth or a believer looking for a home, you are welcome here. Let us walk this path of righteousness together.

Pastor & Pastor (Mrs) Akinboye Michael

church ABOUT THE CHURCH

Our Story & Mission

The Coming King Ministries was founded on June 6, 2004 by the Late Apostle Daniel Olaide Olabosipo Olatoye, beginning with just 11 members in Olorunsogo, Idi-Aba, Abeokuta, Ogun State, Nigeria. By the grace of God, the ministry has grown to over 12 branches, touching lives across Ogun, Oyo States and beyond.
