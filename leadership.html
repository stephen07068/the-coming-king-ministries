General Overseer — The Coming King Ministries

Welcome to The Coming King Ministries. Following the glorious transition of our founder, Late Apostle Olatoye O. Daniel, the Lord has entrusted the leadership of this ministry to us. We stand on the firm foundation laid by our beloved founder and remain committed to the core mandate: preparing saints for the coming King.

Our passion is to see every believer equipped with the Word of God, walking in holiness, and ready for the imminent return of our Lord Jesus Christ. Whether you are joining us for the first time or have been with us for years, you are deeply loved and welcomed.

By the grace of God, we are committed to evangelism, discipleship, sound biblical doctrine, and the expansion of God's Kingdom across Nigeria and beyond. Together, let us run this race with endurance and finish well.

Honouring Our Founder

A life fully surrendered to God's purpose — his legacy lives on in every soul touched by this ministry.

Late Apostle Daniel Olaide
Olabosipo Olatoye

May 21, 1959 – March 24, 2024

Founder, The Coming King Ministries

"A voice in the wilderness, a pillar of truth, and a father to many. He gave his entire life to the proclamation of the Gospel and the preparation of souls for the return of Jesus Christ."

Born on May 21, 1959 in Ijebu-Igbo, Ogun State, Late Apostle Daniel Olaide Olabosipo Olatoye surrendered his life to Jesus Christ in 1980. Called and ordained, he studied at the Evanpentecost Bible College and faithfully served in the Fishers of Men Pentecostal Church before receiving the divine mandate to establish a new work.

On June 6, 2004, he founded The Coming King Ministries with just 11 members, driven by a singular burden: to prepare a generation for the Second Coming of Christ. In 2012, he pioneered the launch of the RockCity FM broadcast ministry — extending the gospel beyond walls. He faithfully served until his glorious transition on March 24, 2024.
