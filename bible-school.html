menu_book ABOUT OUR BIBLE SCHOOL

Rooted in the Word

The Coming King Ministries operates a Publishing Department that produces and distributes Sunday Bible School lessons for all branches. These lessons are designed to ground believers in sound biblical doctrine, deepen their knowledge of the Scriptures, and prepare them for the return of Christ.

Bible School holds every Sunday from 7:45 AM to 9:00 AM, before the main service. All members and visitors are encouraged to attend. Lessons are also published weekly for those who cannot attend in person.
