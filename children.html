child_care ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

Nurturing Young Hearts for Christ

The Children's Department of The Coming King Ministries is dedicated to raising godly children who know and love God from an early age. Through interactive Bible lessons, songs of worship, memory verse challenges, and fun activities, we help children build a strong foundation in faith.

Our goal is to prepare the next generation as vessels ready for the Coming King. Every child is valued and nurtured in a safe, loving environment.
